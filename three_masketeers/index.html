The Three Masketeers

1. 3D-Printed Face Masks (2020-2022)

During the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, The Three Mask-eteers focused on designing and 3D-printing protective face masks for those in need. We donated over 400 masks, each equipped with spare filters, to teachers, first responders, local politicians, and immunocompromised community members.

2. 3D-Printed Face Shields for Rural Hospitals in India (2021)

As the COVID-19 Delta variant surged, we shifted our focus to supporting healthcare workers in rural India by donating 480 3D-printed face shields.

3. RPM Boards for Nonverbal Autistic Children (2022-2023)

We designed and 3D-printed RPM boards, allowing nonverbal autistic children to communicate by pointing to letters to form words and sentences.

4. 3D-Printed Prosthetic Hand for a Venezuelan Refugee (2024)

We created a custom 3D-printed prosthetic hand for a 7-year-old Venezuelan refugee who lost his hand, helping him regain the ability to perform daily tasks.
