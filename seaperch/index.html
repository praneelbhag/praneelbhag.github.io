SeaPerch

1. Founding the SeaPerch and Robotics Club

Driven by a passion for robotics and engineering, I founded the SeaPerch and Robotics Club at my high school to give students hands-on experience in underwater robotics. Starting from scratch, I recruited over 20 members and formed 4 competitive teams. I organized regular meetings, workshops, and hands-on projects where students developed technical skills in engineering, coding, and robotics.

In addition to building a strong team, I focused on outreach by conducting volunteer workshops at local elementary and middle schools, inspiring younger students to explore STEM through fun, hands-on activities. This not only prepared them for future competitions but also fostered a passion for science and technology within our community.
